d3bate

A digital debating toolkit

Go to app

d3bate is a digital debating toolkit. It is developed entirely as open source software (under a Mozilla Public License 2.0) and you can host it entirely yourself should you wish to do so. A publicly hosted version is also provided.

d3bate allows you to:

  • quickly schedule events in your club's calendar (planned)
  • hold debating sessions over livestream, including: (planned)
    • run training sessions (with one presenter and multiple listeners)
    • run debates (with multiple debaters and a judge) and allow teams to prep their debates in advance

Keep track of the development of the application on our Github page.
